Welcome

Water quality is impacted by increasing urbanization, population growth, economic growth, and massive changes in environmental conditions. One of the biggest threats to the sustainability and availability of freshwater is the deterioration of water quality. IIT Ropar and AWADH are cosponsoring this first International Conference on Water Technologies and Cyber-Physical Systems; to provide a unique international forum to exchange scientific knowledge and experiences and present new findings to integrated water technologies and cyber-physical systems. The conference aims to facilitate networking opportunities between scientists, managers,engineers, and stakeholders on challenges and solutions to water-based technologies.
